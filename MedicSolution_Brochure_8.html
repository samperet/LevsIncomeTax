From integrated room-in-room operating theatres to ultra-mobile crisis platforms, every MedicSolution product is engineered to the same Swedish standard, with single-supplier accountability from design through commissioning.

InsideTM
Prefab critical-care rooms, integrated within the building
Operating theatres, ICU and sterile rooms manufactured and tested in the factory, then lifted into the hospital structure. Up to 40% faster than on-site builds for critical environments.
Best for:New hospital builds  ·  OR theatres, ICU, CSSD  ·  Cleanrooms ISO 5 to 8
Add-OnTM
Prefab extension attached to existing facilities
Turnkey expansion module bolted to or placed adjacent to an existing hospital. Continues operations during renovations or capacity shortages. Permanent or temporary, rent or purchase.
Best for:Renovations  ·  Evacuation cover  ·  Capacity shortages  ·  Diagnostics (CT/MRI/X-ray)
Stand-AloneTM
Freestanding healthcare buildings for long-term operation
Fully prefabricated, scalable, independent buildings with 50+ year service life. Self-contained capacity for new regional clinical hubs, with the option to expand, refurbish or relocate later.
